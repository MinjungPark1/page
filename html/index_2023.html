👋 Hey there, I'm Minjung

I'm a first year Ph.D. student in Human-Computer Interaction Institute at Carnegie Mellon University
advised by John Zimmerman and Jodi Forlizzi.

My research interests are focused on Human-AI Interaction, with a particular emphasis on bringing human-centered design to complex intelligent systems to foster interactive and collaborative communities among humans, communities, and agents.
Before, I completed my B.S. and M.S. in Industrial Design at KAIST.
Previously, @ Color Lab + CIXD Lab + CIDR Lab + CJ Livecity

I am passionate about have human-centric approach towards technology problems that enable people to live empowered lives. If you are interested in collaborating, send me an 📩 email with your interests!

Besides my research, I've actually put together a bucket list for my balanced and fulfilling life. It's all about checking off those exciting goals and experiences that I've always wanted to try!🙋🏻‍♀️
